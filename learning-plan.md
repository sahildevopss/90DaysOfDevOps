# 90 Days DevOps Learning Plan

## My Understanding of DevOps & Cloud Engineering

DevOps is a culture and engineering practice that focuses on automation, collaboration, continuous integration, continuous deployment, monitoring, and faster software delivery. Cloud Engineering helps organizations build scalable and reliable infrastructure using cloud platforms and automation tools.

I believe DevOps is not only about tools but also about problem-solving, ownership, and continuous improvement.

---

## Why I Am Learning DevOps & Cloud

I am currently working as a Security Analyst with around 6 years of experience in cybersecurity. I want to transition into the DevOps and Cloud domain to build strong infrastructure, automation, and deployment skills.

My goal is to become a skilled DevOps Engineer who can work on Linux, Cloud, CI/CD pipelines, Docker, Kubernetes, Infrastructure as Code, and automation tools.

I also want to improve my career growth, technical exposure, and salary opportunities in the cloud and DevOps industry.

---

## My 90-Day Goals

1. Build strong fundamentals in Linux, Git, Networking, and Shell Scripting.
2. Learn Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD, and Cloud basics through hands-on practice.
3. Deploy and manage real-world projects using DevOps tools and document my learning on GitHub.

---

## Core DevOps Skills I Want to Build

- Linux troubleshooting and system administration
- CI/CD pipeline implementation and automation
- Docker and Kubernetes deployment/debugging

---

## Weekly Time Commitment

- Weekdays: 2 to 2.5 hours daily
- Weekends: 4 to 6 hours daily

I will focus on consistency, hands-on practice, GitHub activity, and daily learning updates throughout this 90-day journey.

---

## My Commitment

For the next 90 days, I will stay disciplined, avoid excuses, and continuously improve my skills step by step. I will maintain consistency even on difficult days and focus on becoming industry-ready for DevOps and Cloud Engineering roles.
